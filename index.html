About Us

VeganBite is a student-built restaurant concept focused on fresh, modern, meat-free comfort food.

Our Story

VeganBite began as a small weekend pop-up serving veggie burgers and fresh lemonade to neighbors who wanted food that felt good and tasted exciting. People kept asking for more options, so the team built a full menu around plants, grains, beans, fruits, herbs, and bold sauces.

Today, VeganBite is known for Green Goddess Burgers, Rainbow Rice & Beans Bowls, Garden Veggie Pizza, Hummus Veggie Rolls, and dairy-free desserts. Our mission is to prove that meat-free food can be comforting, satisfying, and fun for everyone.
